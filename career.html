An employer that shares your values

Navyug Foundation serves as a convener, thought leader, and voice of the NGO community. Our talented staff are dedicated to advancing the NGO sector to ultimately make the world a more peaceful, just, and prosperous place. Our teams are experts at organizing meaningful, action-oriented conversations that influence and advance global development and humanitarian policy and practice. Navyug Foundation is seeking individuals at all levels who excel at supporting and facilitating coalition building. Navyug Foundation is committed to workplace diversity and inclusion and is an equal opportunity employer.

You’ll discover an inspiring and friendly place to work, with new challenges and opportunities each day. Our people are the heart of our business, and we reward and recognise the great work they do. So whatever your background and skills, chances are we’ll have a job for you; an opportunity for you to grow and develop, while helping us to make giving count.

As an equal opportunity employer, we seek to recruit people regardless of race, religion, gender, age, sexuality and disability.

Life at Navyug Foundation

Navyug Foundation aims to attract and retain a highly talented workforce, which will help the organisation achieve its vision and mission. We are a equal opportunity employer and encourage diversity within the organisation.

  • No 09:00AM – 5:00PM work timing, early risers, and night owls get to work in their most productive hours
  • No dress code, casual / formal / party wear / ethnic wear / all blacks and blues / doesn’t matter – we wear what makes us happy
  • No limits on leaves, we decide when we want to take off to rejuvenate and celebrate based on our priorities
  • No judgements, everyone is accepted and we are inclusive of every single opinion, belief, and idea.
  • Get one extra day off for volunteer work, to be used for centrally organized good cause events, or for your own initiatives.
  • We highly value knowledge exchange and encourage to invest in personal development.
