Donate for Bihar Flood Relief Fund!

The recent floods in Bihar have left countless families devastated, particularly in the districts bordering Nepal, including Sitamarhi, Madhubani, Supaul, Araria, Madhepura, Purnia and Kishanganj. The relentless rains have caused rivers to overflow, submerging homes, destroying crops, and disrupting lives. The situation is dire, with entire villages under water, people stranded without basic necessities, and many forced to leave their homes in search of safety.

The Current Situation

This catastrophic flood has affected over a million people, with many districts still reeling under the impact. Roads have been washed away, making relief efforts even more challenging. Families are struggling without access to clean water, food, or shelter. Health risks are rising with waterborne diseases spreading rapidly. The situation is urgent, and the need for immediate action is critical.

How Navyug Foundation is Helping

At Navyug Foundation, we have stepped in to provide critical relief to those affected. Our team is on the ground distributing emergency supplies, including:

  • Food packets and clean drinking water to ensure no one goes hungry or thirsty.
  • Temporary shelters to provide safe spaces for families who have lost their homes.
  • Medical camps to address immediate health concerns and prevent the spread of diseases.
  • Clothing and hygiene kits to maintain basic dignity and health during these trying times.

But we cannot do this alone. The scale of the disaster is vast, and we need your support to reach more people.

Why Your Donation Matters

Your donation can make a huge difference in the lives of those suffering in the aftermath of this flood. Every contribution, no matter how small, helps us provide life-saving resources to more families. With your help, we can:

  • Expand our reach to more villages and communities.
  • Provide long-term support for rehabilitation and rebuilding efforts.
  • Ensure that children and families can survive these tough times and look towards a brighter future.

How You Can Help

Donate today to the Bihar Flood Relief Fund and help us bring hope to the flood-affected families. Your generosity can provide a lifeline to those who need it the most.

Together, we can make a difference. Let’s stand by Bihar in this time of crisis and help rebuild lives.
