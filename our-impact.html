Story About Us

If you want to make a change in people's life, start from yourself.

The small village was plagued with poverty and lack of education, until a group of dedicated individuals started an NGO to provide basic necessities and educational opportunities to the residents. The community slowly began to thrive and the NGO continued to grow, making a lasting impact on the village and its inhabitants.

A registered Trust, Navyug Foundation began its journey in 2015 with a small group of marginalised children and women in the slums of Delhi with a vision to provide high-quality holistic education and livelihood skills to enable positive community transformation.
