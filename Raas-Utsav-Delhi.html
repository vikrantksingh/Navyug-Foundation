Raas Utsav - A Dandiya Night

Dandiya Night is a traditional Indian festival event that celebrates the rich cultural heritage of India through music, dance, and food. This event will feature lively dandiya and garba dances, engaging performances, a variety of traditional and contemporary cuisines, and interactive activities for all age groups. Our goal is to bring together people from diverse backgrounds to experience the joy and unity that our culture offers.

Why should you attend?

  • Cultural Fun: Dive into vibrant Indian traditions with colorful dance and music.
  • Social Joy: Meet new people and enjoy a lively, communal atmosphere.
  • Energetic Vibes: Feel the excitement with rhythmic beats and inclusive dance fun.


🎶 Highlights of the Night:

  • Dandiya Dance: Enjoy the classic rhythms and twirls of this traditional Garba dance, perfect for all ages and experience levels.
  • Live Music: Groove to the beats of our talented DJ, bringing you the best of Bollywood and traditional tunes.
  • Themed Decor: Immerse yourself in the festive spirit with our beautifully decorated venue, featuring traditional motifs and vibrant colors.
  • Delicious Cuisine: Savor a delightful selection of Indian snacks and sweets to keep you energized throughout the night.
  • Costume Contest: Dress to impress in your most colorful and creative traditional attire for a chance to win exciting prizes!

    • Whether you're a seasoned dancer or new to Dandiya, this event is the perfect opportunity to celebrate, connect with friends, and make new memories. Bring your family, invite your friends, and let's make this a night to remember!
