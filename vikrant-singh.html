About

Vikrant, a seasoned executive with over 15 years in the IT industry, currently serves as Engagement Director at Infosys, a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting. In his role, Vikrant plays a crucial part in guiding clients through transformative journeys, utilizing Infosys's expertise in consulting, industry solutions, business process services, and managed services.

Passionate about innovation, customer success, and speed to market, Vikrant brings a dynamic approach to his work. As an MBA graduate, he combines academic rigor with practical expertise. Additionally, Vikrant holds multiple certifications in project management and agile methodologies, further enhancing his ability to navigate the evolving landscape of the IT sector. Vikrant's comprehensive experience, strategic acumen, and commitment to excellence make him a valuable asset in driving success for clients and contributing to Infosys's position as a leader in the industry.

As a Social worker, he is helping people to solve and cope with problems in their everyday lives. He is working with groups, community organizations, and policymakers to develop or improve programs, services, policies, and social conditions.
